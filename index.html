Research

This research group focuses on the development enhanced molecular dynamics techniques for studying physical systems. We are interested in enhanced sampling methods for predicting kinetics and thermodynamics of complex systems using atomistic simulations, statistical mechanics and machine learning.

The research is led by Jakub Rydzewski, within the Department of Biophysics at the Institute of Physics, Nicolaus Copernicus University in Torun, Poland.
